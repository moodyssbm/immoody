A New Look

3-14-17

This was long, long overdue. I've always enjoyed the aesthetic properties of minimalism, but I've always felt that my site was TOO minimalistic. So I've switched to this new, darker theme. I wanted to keep a minimalistic mindset and stray away from any sort of complext patterns or images, but I wanted to beef up the site as well so that it would feel like I have more content on each page. I'm happy with this grey and pink design if I'm being honest. It didn't take very long, but I'm happy I finally got around to updating the site.
